# Financial-Dashboard
group project for the "software Engineering" course in JCT

## About this project
Personal Portfolio Copilot is an educational investment platform designed to help beginner investors in Israel build and manage their investment portfolios with confidence. Our mission is to make long-term investing simple, clear, and accessible to everyone.

## We provide:
* Personalized investment plans based on your goals and risk profile
* Weekly actionable recommendations delivered every Sunday
* Long-term scenarios showing potential outcomes over 10, 15, and 40 years
* Educational support with plain-English explanations for every decision
* Practice mode to learn without risking real money
